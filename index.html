What Makes Pomelli Different from a Normal Design Tool

Pomelli doesn't just generate content — it understands your brand's identity and creates everything in your unique style.

1

Analyzes Your Website Instantly

Enter your business URL and Pomelli scans your site to extract your brand's unique identity — colors, fonts, tone of voice, and visual style.

2

Business DNA Profile

Your brand identity is compiled into a structured "Business DNA" profile that captures everything that makes your business visually and tonally unique.

3

Generates Campaigns Automatically

Based on your Business DNA, Pomelli suggests tailored campaign ideas and generates high-quality, branded creatives ready for publishing.

4

Edit, Download, and Publish

Fine-tune the generated assets directly in the platform, then export them for use across social media, ads, and marketing channels.
